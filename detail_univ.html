University of the Philippines

  • Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering
  • Public State University funded by the Philippine government.
  • Civil Engineering program focuses on designing, constructing, and maintaining infrastructures such as roads, bridges, and buildings.
  • Civil Engineering at UP trains students in planning, designing, and constructing infrastructure projects with strong foundations in mathematics, physics, and environmental studies.

Campus Branches:

  • UP Diliman
  • UP Los Baños

Tuition and Fees Info

  • Free tuition for qualified students under the Free Higher Education Law. Minor miscellaneous and laboratory fees may apply.

Admission Requirements:

Freshman Applicants

1. Take the UP College Admission Test

  • Must take and pass the UPCAT to qualify for admission to UP, including Civil Engineering.
  • UPCAT is administered once a year (usually early August).
  • The test has subtests in Language, Reading, Science, and Math. Scores are combined with your high school grades to determine qualification.

2. Minimum Qualifications to Apply for UPCAT

  • To be eligible for the UPCAT:
    • You must be graduating from senior high school (Grade 12) or be a graduate.
    • You should have complete grades for Grades 8–11 (K–12).
    • You must not have taken college subjects yet.
    • You cannot have taken UPCAT or completed a UP college application before.

3. Required Documents for UPCAT Application

  • Generally submitted through the UPCAT online portal:
    • Form 1 – Personal Data Sheet (online submission)
    • Form 2B – High School Record (uploaded later)
    • A valid email address used throughout the application process (Official photocopies and certificates may be needed for final validation).

4. Additional Documents After Passing UPCAT

  • If you qualify for admission and a slot in BS Civil Engineering:
    • Official high school transcript (original copy)
    • Birth Certificate (original and photocopy)
    • Medical Certificate from UP Health Service
    • Student Directory copies
    • Passport size photos
    • These are common for enrollment processing once admitted.
